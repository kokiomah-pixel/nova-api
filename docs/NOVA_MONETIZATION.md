# Sharpe Nova OS -- Monetization

## Pricing aligns with capital governed, not API usage

Sharpe Nova OS operates as decision infrastructure.

API calls measure interaction. Nova conditions capital before execution.

Value emerges from capital being evaluated and constrained before deployment, not from requests processed.

The economic model reflects what is governed, not what is computed.

---

## Decision Activity (Entry Layer)

Nova access is defined by decision activity.

Each call represents capital being evaluated before execution.

This layer allows systems to validate decision behavior under real conditions.

Decision discipline becomes operational, measurable, and applied before capital moves.

## Infrastructure Layer

Beyond API access, Nova functions as embedded decision architecture.

Organizations integrate Nova into:

- capital allocation systems
- risk frameworks
- execution pathways

At this layer, measurement shifts.

The relevant metric is no longer decision volume.

It is the scale of capital Nova conditions before deployment.

## Capital Layer

At scale, Nova becomes structural.

It governs capital allocation at the portfolio level.

This includes:

- deployment decisions
- risk exposure
- strategic resource distribution

Value is measured in capital conditioned before execution.

### Pricing Model

Pricing reflects economic alignment.

It is based on assets under governance.

This is not a transaction fee.

It reflects Nova's role in improving the quality of capital deployment, with value scaling alongside capital governed.

---

## Final Principle

Pricing reflects function.

Sharpe Nova OS does not sell computation.

It applies decision discipline to capital before execution.
