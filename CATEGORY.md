# Sharpe Nova OS — Category Definition

## The Shift

Capital is becoming autonomous.

Agents now execute faster than humans can reason.

---

## The Problem

As execution improves, the bottleneck shifts:

From execution -> to decision coherence

Errors now compound faster under:
- leverage
- automation
- continuous markets

---

## The Missing Layer

There is no system ensuring that decisions remain coherent before execution.

---

## The Solution

Sharpe Nova OS:

A **verifiable decision discipline layer** that conditions capital decisions before they are executed.

---

## What It Enables

- Consistent decision quality across agents
- Reduction of repeated failure patterns
- IC-defensible reasoning
- Verifiable decision integrity

---

## Where It Fits

```text
[ Agents / Strategies ]
            ↓
[ Nova OS ]
            ↓
[ Execution ]
```

---

## Final Statement

Nova does not move capital.

Nova determines whether capital **should move, and under what constraints**.
